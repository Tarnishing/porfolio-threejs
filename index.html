My name is Nikola Petreski, and I am a Full Stack web developer with over five years of experience with UI design. I take great pride in the actual process of creating a beautiful and optimized application that brings joy to the users who may incorporate it as a part of their life. I come from a multidisciplinary background in management, policy, and natural sciences. It was there that I realized just how interconnected the world of computing is to the rest of the world.

My personal favorite technology stack is ReactJS for the front end, with MongoDB and NodeJS/Express to fulfill backend needs. Furthermore, for solo projects I feel like I am able to get a lot of value through just React and FireBase. I am also familiar with using Django and Flask to build backends, with some experience building graphical user interfaces with Python.
